About Course

I have met a lot of students who tell me "I really want to know Machine Learning, but I am getting stuck with the math part." Well, that is why I decided to create a unique course covering math and programming topics which will make it easier for you to learn ML. I have used lots of real-life examples to explain the underlying concepts in a completely hands-on way. You will also learn Numpy and Matplotlib along with the math, to cover the programming aspects and visually understand the concepts.
